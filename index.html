Carrot Pie

Love carrot muffins? Then you HAVE to try these healthy carrot pie ! Moist, tender, and warmly spiced, it's a delicious breakfast or on-the-go snack.

Breakfast

These carrot pie combine two of my favorite things: carrot cake and muffins! It's exactly what I want to eat on a spring morning (or any morning, really). It's moist, warmly spiced, and studded with chewy raisins and crunchy walnuts. Seriously, who wouldn't want to start their day with one of these? If you need a little last-minute something to round out your Easter menu tomorrow, I think these carrot pie would be perfect. But if you don't celebrate Easter, or if your menu is all set to go, I hope you'll find a time to make them anyway.
